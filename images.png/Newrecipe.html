Banana Shake

Ingredients

  1. Ripend Bananas 6
  2. Milk one litre
  3. Honey/Sugar According to your taste
  4. Dry fruits optional

Method

  1. Peel all the bananas
  2. Cut them in small chunks .
  3. Put them in a mixer grinder. Add milk .
  4. Run the mixer for two to three minutes.
  5. Pour the shake into the glasses . On the top add some dry fruits in small pieces
